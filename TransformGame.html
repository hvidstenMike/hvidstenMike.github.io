In the window below we see Geo trying to get to his birdhouse home.  At the left of the window, there are buttons depicting simple transformations.  By clicking on these buttons we can make Geo move by that transformation.  See if you can help Geo to get to his house and see if you can do it in the fewest number of steps, as he is tired out from doing so much geometry! 

As you move Geo around, a running list of the transformations you have used appears to the left of the window.  Note that these are listed as they are applied to Geo - they are applied from the back of the list to the front. 

For a new example, click "New". To undo a transformation, click "Undo."  To see a solution (not guaranteed to be optimal!), click "Show."  Every solution is possible using at most 5 transformations. See if you can do it in less!
