Exploring Geometry

Semester Sample Syllabus
  • 14 weeks - meet M,T,Th,F;  52 class days (Take off four days for breaks)
  • Cover Chapters 1, 2, 4, 5.1-5.6, 7(only Poincare model, skip Project 11), 9(some of the first four sections)
  • Lab day works best on Friday
  • Two hour exams (on Chapters 1,2 and 4,5)
  • Mini-Projects are done in the classroom in groups of three
  • Final Project on a topic related to the course
  • No Final Exam
  • Writing Exercises:

    •     Daily Homework (Proofs)
        Lab Project Reports
        Final Project Paper
    Detailed Syllabus
    Week 1 (1/2 week): Greeks, Project 1 on Golden Ratio,

    Week 2: Axiomatic Method (1.4-1.6), Project 2

    Week 3: Basic Euclidean Geometry(2.1-2.2), Mini-Project 2.4.1, Project 3 on Triangles

    Week 4: Measurement and Similarity (2.4-2.5), Mini-Project 2.5.1, Circle Geometry (2.6)

    Week 5: Project 4 on Circle Inversion, Review, Exam on Chapters 1 and 2

    Week 6: Euclidean Constructions (4.1), Project 6,  Constructibility(Briefly, 4.3), Mini-Project 4.4 on Origami

    Week 7: Isometries, Reflections, Translations (5.1-5.3), Mini-Project on Isometries

    Fall Break

    Week 8: Rotations (5.4), Glide Reflections (5.6),
    Discussion of Final Projects, Choose Topics

    Week 9: Representation of Isometries (5.7.1-5.7.2),
    Project 7 on Quilts

    Week 10: Compositions of Isometries (5.7.3-5.7.5), Symmetry Groups (6.1), Project 8 on Compositions

    Week 11: Exam 2 on Chapters 4 and 5, Intro to Non-Euclidean Geometry, Models of Hyperbolic
    Geometry (7.2),      Library Research Day

    Week 13: Triangles in Hyperbolic Geometry (7.5.2), Area in Hyperbolic Geometry (7.6), Fractals and Self-Similarity (9.1-9.2), Draft of Final Project Due

    Thanksgiving Break
