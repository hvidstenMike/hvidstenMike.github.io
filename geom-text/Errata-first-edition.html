Exploring Geometry

Errata (First Edition, First Printing)

This web page lists errors that have been discovered in the first edition of the text Geometry with Geometry Explorer, by Mike Hvidsten, published by McGraw-Hill, January 2005.  Please note that any changes involving mathematical notation are expressed in TeX form.
If you discover errors other than those listed, please email them to hvidsten(at)gustavus.edu

 Def 2.5
 Many authors define supplementary in terms of angle measure, and use the term "linear pair" for Def 2.5.  The author wished to keep the angle definitions free from language of angle measure, and still keep the more familiar notion of supplementarity, thus the motivation for the definition as stated.
